Kelly Rhodes
Vice President

Email: vicepresident@ocsoccerclub.org

What makes all the rainy practices, early Saturday games, and occasional referee disagreements (always respectful, of course!) worth it? It's seeing the pure joy on kids' faces when they connect with the ball just right, or when teammates celebrate together after working hard. Soccer taught me about teamwork and perseverance as a kid, and now I get to watch those same valuable lessons take root in a new generation – starting with my own soccer-loving daughters.
Between board duties, coaching responsibilities, and being my girls' biggest fan on the sidelines, soccer isn't just a sport in our household – it's practically a family member. And thankfully, it's one that doesn't leave dirty dishes in the sink!
