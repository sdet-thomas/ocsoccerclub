Our High School Coed program welcomes all high school students, providing a fun, competitive, and inclusive soccer experience. Teams play against local clubs and other Oregon City teams. Note: Players on high school teams cannot participate in OCSC recreational coed teams due to Soccer 5 Rules.

We participate in the Soccer 5 League which serves the communities of Aurora, Canby, Clackamas, Colton, Damascus, Estacada, Gladstone, Hubbard, Lake Oswego, Milwaukie, Molalla, North Clackamas, Oregon City, West Linn, & Wilsonville.

Practices

Practice schedules are set by each coach. Players will be contacted after team assignments. Bringing your own ball is recommended but not required.

Age Group Format

  • 9th & 10th Grade: 11v11 games
  • 11th & 12th Grade: 11v11 games

Playing Up: Allowed one age group up if space is available. No player may play up two divisions.

Playing Down: Not permitted. Players must play in their school year division.
