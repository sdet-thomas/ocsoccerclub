Welcome to the Little Pioneers, our in-house soccer program for Pre-K through 2nd grade! This program is designed to introduce young players to soccer in a fun, supportive, and community-focused environment. All games are held locally in Oregon City, and we encourage early registration to secure your spot.

Practices

Coaches will contact parents after the coaches' meeting to share practice details. Practices are held once per week during both Spring and Fall. Players are encouraged to bring their own size 3 ball, but it is not required.

Age Group Format

  • PK & Kinder Coed: 3v3 games
  • 1st & 2nd Grade: 4v4 games

Playing Up: Allowed one age group up if space is available. No player may play up two divisions.

Playing Down: Not permitted. Players must play in their age-appropriate division.

Uniforms

  • Red Jersey
  • Black Shorts (any brand)
  • Black Socks (any brand)
  • Official uniforms available at Tursi Soccer
  • All players must wear the club-approved uniform and bring their full kit to each game.
