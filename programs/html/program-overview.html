Discover the range of youth soccer programs offered by OCSC, designed for all ages and skill levels. Our club is dedicated to fostering fun, safety, and growth through soccer, teamwork, and community spirit.

  • Recreational Soccer (3rd - 8th Grade)
  • High School Coed Soccer (9th - 12th Grade)

Coaching Opportunities

We rely on dedicated parent volunteers to coach our teams. Interested in making a difference? Register as a coach and help shape the next generation of players!

Team Assignments

Teams are formed by grade and school, with an emphasis on community and friendship. While we try to honor requests, placement is not guaranteed, especially for late registrations. New members are assigned based on space and requests when possible.
