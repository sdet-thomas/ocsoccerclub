Oregon City Soccer Club Programs Overview

Welcome to Oregon City Soccer Club! We offer a variety of programs for youth of all ages and skill levels. Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and inclusive environment for players to learn and enjoy soccer, make new friends, and develop life skills through teamwork and sportsmanship.

Player Registration

All players must be registered in accordance with the rules of the club, outlined in the Registration Policy. To register your player, click here.

Coaches

Oregon City Soccer Club will field as many teams as possible, as long as there are enough parent volunteers to coach them. Interested in coaching? Register here.

Recreation coaches are all volunteers that dedicate significant amounts of time to the team, and we are grateful for all of our coaches. Our program works because of our volunteers.

Team Assignments

OCSC has registrants from all over the city which allows players to meet new friends and stay in the community which they attend school. Teams will be formed based on grade when possible. In order to form teams some players may be put on a mixed grade team (no more than one grade - players may not play down). Players are reassigned to the same roster unless we are asked not to do so. However, because a player was on a certain team last year doesn’t guarantee that same placement if the registration is late. New Club members are assigned to teams based on space, the school attended and if possible, teammate requests.

During the registration process we do allow for parents to “request” players or coaches. Parent coaches always have their child on their team. We want to stress that although you may have requested a teammate or coach, we will NOT guarantee to accommodate your request.
