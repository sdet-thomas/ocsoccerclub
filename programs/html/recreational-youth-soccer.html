Our recreational program is designed for 3rd–8th graders who want to enjoy soccer, develop skills, and play with friends in a supportive, non-travel environment. All games are local and focus on fun, teamwork, and growth.

We participate in the Soccer 5 League which serves the communities of Aurora, Canby, Clackamas, Colton, Damascus, Estacada, Gladstone, Hubbard, Lake Oswego, Milwaukie, Molalla, North Clackamas, Oregon City, West Linn, & Wilsonville.

Practices

Practices are held twice per week. Coaches will contact parents with details after team assignments. Ball sizes: 8–12 years use size 4, 13+ use size 5.

Season Details

  • Cost: $143 (includes registration, field maintenance, and parks fees)
  • Games: All games on Saturdays, Sept 6–Oct 25

Age Group Format

  • 3rd & 4th Grade: 7v7 games
  • 5th & 6th Grade: 9v9 games
  • 7th & 8th Grade: 11v11 games

Playing Up: Allowed one age group up if space is available. No player may play up two divisions.

Playing Down: Not permitted. Players must play in their age-appropriate division.

Uniforms

  • Red Jersey
  • White Jersey
  • Black Shorts
  • Black Socks (any brand)
  • Home: Red, Away: White
  • Official uniforms at Tursi Soccer
  • All players must wear the club-approved uniform and bring their full kit to each game.
